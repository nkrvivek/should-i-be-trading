## Screenshot hygiene (HARD rules)

- Scrub: account numbers, NAV totals beyond the position shown, names/emails,
  broker session tokens in URLs.
- Never show another person's account (bildof partner book = never posted).
- P&L screenshots show the position's numbers only, not account aggregates.
- Product screenshots (SIBT) may show demo data until customer flows ship.
