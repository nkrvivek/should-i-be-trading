# SIBT Go-To-Market Plan

## Positioning

SIBT should not be sold as a generic "AI stock research platform."

That market is crowded and hard to differentiate. The clearer positioning is:

**SIBT is an explainable trade decision engine for active stock traders.**

Core promise:

- Know when to trade
- Know when to wait
- See why the verdict changed

Suggested one-line positioning:

- "An explainable trade score for active stock traders."
- "See TRADE, CAUTION, or AVOID before you enter."
- "Market regime plus ticker-specific signals in one fast verdict."

## Best Initial ICP

Start with one primary customer:

- U.S. self-directed swing traders
- Trading equities and earnings setups
- Uses TradingView, StockTwits, Reddit, broker tools, or Discord groups
- Wants faster decisions, not deeper spreadsheets

Do not lead with:

- passive investors
- retirees
- long-horizon portfolio allocators

Those users do not feel the same urgency around timing and setup quality.

## Product Wedge

Compete on decision clarity, not chart depth or raw data breadth.

What SIBT should own:

- trade verdicts
- market regime context
- catalyst-aware trade selection
- explainability
- risk framing before entry

What other tools already own:

- TradingView: charting and alerting
- Koyfin: broad research coverage
- OptionStrat: options strategy visualization

## Core Lead Sources

Prioritize these in order:

1. Short-form social content
   - X
   - YouTube Shorts
   - TikTok if you want broader top-of-funnel

2. High-intent SEO pages
   - ticker pages
   - earnings setup pages
   - insider buying pages
   - regime pages

3. Smaller trading creators and newsletter partners
   - do not start with the biggest accounts
   - start with credible niche creators who have trust with active traders

4. Community distribution
   - Reddit
   - StockTwits
   - Discord communities
   - niche trader Slack/Telegram groups

5. Email
   - daily or 3x/week setup recap
   - "Top TRADE / Top AVOID / Regime check"

## Messaging Angles That Should Convert

Use:

- "Before you enter a trade, get a verdict."
- "9 signals. 1 score. Clear explanation."
- "Avoid forcing trades in bad market regimes."
- "See whether the market and the ticker agree."

Avoid:

- "Institutional-grade"
- "next-generation AI research"
- "beat the market with AI"
- "all-in-one terminal"

Those are vague and overclaimed.

## Homepage Rewrite Direction

Your homepage hero should answer 3 things immediately:

1. Who is this for?
   - active stock traders

2. What do I get?
   - TRADE / CAUTION / AVOID

3. Why is it different?
   - regime + ticker + catalyst + sentiment + insider signals in one explainable score

Suggested hero:

**Know when to trade. Know when to wait.**

Get an explainable TRADE / CAUTION / AVOID verdict from market regime, earnings, insider activity, social sentiment, and ticker quality in one view.

Suggested sub-CTA pair:

- `Start Free Trial`
- `See Today's Top Setups`

## 30-Day Content System

Post every market day using this format:

1. Pre-market post
   - market regime
   - best 3 setups
   - 1 avoid name

2. Midday post
   - one live example of score shift
   - why it changed

3. After-close post
   - what worked
   - what stayed caution/avoid
   - one learning point

## Funnel

Keep the funnel simple:

1. Content or SEO page
2. Click into a live ticker or screener view
3. Capture email or start free trial
4. Send recurring verdict-driven emails

## What To Measure

Track:

- visitor to trial rate
- visitor to email capture rate
- landing page to ticker-page CTR
- X post to site CTR
- best converting message angle
- best converting screenshot type

## Best Screenshot Angles

The images most likely to convert are:

- a ranked composite screener table
- a ticker header showing `TRADE` or `AVOID`
- order review with verdict visible before execution
- social sentiment + composite badge on the same ticker
- regime dashboard plus a "do not force trades" takeaway
